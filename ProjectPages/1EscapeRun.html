Escape Run game is an endless runner 3D video game. Escape Run game is only for Android users. The game starts by clicking on the PLAY button, while Veer (the game's starter character) or any other character plucks the mangoes from the tree, and then gets caught in the act by the gardener, who starts chasing the character. While running, the player can swipe up, down, left, or right to avoid crashing into oncoming obstacles especially moving buses and road barriers. By swiping rapidly as speed increases, more points can be acquired. A crash results in a game over. The player can collect various items such as coins, score doublers, coin doublers, and magnets. Shields, rockets, score doublers, coin doublers, and magnets are the boosters which provide the power to the player.
